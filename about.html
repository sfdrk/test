HISTORY

Establishment and Initial Vision

  • BAMX was founded as an entrepreneurial company focused on neighborhood-oriented regeneration, guided by the experience of BAMX Engineering Development Group. The vision emphasized unlocking “hidden treasures,” promoting entrepreneurship, and fostering thoughtful decision-making.

2023

Community-Centric Initiatives

  • BAMX launched its first projects in neighborhoods, prioritizing participation over ownership and utilizing local resources. The company provided cultural, religious, economic, and social services to the community, ensuring transparency and sustainable development.

2024

Expansion and Stock Market Entry

  • BAMX expanded its operations, aiming to become a leading entrepreneurial developer in the stock market. The company continued to re-create worn out, unstable, and historical structures with a focus on sustainability and community involvement.

2025

Leadership in Neighborhood-Oriented Regeneration

  • BAMX positioned itself as a key player in neighborhood regeneration, collaborating with various stakeholders and striving to be the largest private developer contributing to the revitalization of historical structures in the country.

2026

Future Aspirations:

  • Sustained Growth: BAMX plans to maintain its growth trajectory by continually engaging with local communities, leveraging modern engineering techniques, and fostering sustainable development practices.
  • New opportunities for investors and developers
